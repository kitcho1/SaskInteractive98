CAREER AREAS BY EMPLOYER TYPE

AGRI-FOOD INDUSTRY (Private Sector)

  • Technical sales of agricultural inputs.
  • Product testing and development.
  • Food and beverage processing.
  • Credit officers and managers.
  • Consulting firms.
  • Communications.

GOVERNMENT (Public Sector)

  • Regulatory officers.
  • Program administrators.
  • Research assistants and associates.
  • Information officers.

SELF-EMPLOYMENT

  • Farming - about 15% of B.S.A. graduates farm by choice.
  • Contracting and consulting.
  • Agriculture processing and marketing firms.

SAMPLE CAREERS AND EMPLOYERS

  • Research Officers - Agriculture Canada, Plant Genetic Systems, Crop Development Centre
  • Technical Sales - Monsanto, DowElanco, Zeneca Agro
  • Farm Lending - Farm Credit Corporation, all Banks
  • Grain Marketing - Cargill, Pioneer Grain, United Grain Growers
  • Food Inspection - Intercontinental Packers, Burns Food
  • International Development - IDRC, CIDA, World Bank, CUSO
  • Further Studies - M.Sc., Ph.D.
