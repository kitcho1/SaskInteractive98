Prince Albert National Park is not regulated by Saskatchewan Fishing Regulations. Contact Parks Canada for angling limits and licence requirements.

You may not fish in closed waters, or during a closed season. The province is divided into three fishing zones; southern, central and northern. The Angler's Guide outlines the fishing season dates for each fishing area. In general, the southern region opens a few weeks ahead of the central region, which is open about a week in advance of the northern region. Check with the Angler's Guide for details on the fishing season (which varies throughout the province).
