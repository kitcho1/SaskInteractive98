Private Industry Aquaculture

Lake Diefenbaker in the middle of the prairies is home to over 800,000 rainbow trout. But, don't think you can catch them with your rod and reel.

There is a business enterprise here called "commercial aquaculture" or "fish farming". The fish are the 'livestock' of the farm. Aquaculture is which is quickly growing popularity around the world. Countries such as China and Japan practise "fish farming" on a very large scale.
