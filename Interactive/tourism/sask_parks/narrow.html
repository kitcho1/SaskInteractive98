Narrow Hills Provincial Park

GPS: 54d 01.56m N 104d 37.65m W 1721 feet
Location: 129 kms northeast of Prince Albert on Hanson Lake Road
Narrow Hills Provincial Park semi-wilderness territory awaits year round. This is another provincial park located within the Northern Shores tourism region. The park is 53,610 hectares and may be accessed from Highway 106 north of Smeaton or Highway 120 north of Meath Park. There are many stocked lakes at which you can fish, hike, swim, cycle, snowshoe, ski, and snowmobile. Don't miss the Ranger tower and the Narrow Hills Esker, a remnant of the last glacial period.
