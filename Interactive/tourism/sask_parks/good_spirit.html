Good Spirit Lake Provincial Park

GPS: 51d 30.49m N 102d 39.93m W 1594 feet
Location: 50 kms north of Yorkton on #47
One of the best natural beaches of Saskatchewan awaits you at the south end of Good Spirit Lake. Within Kelsey Country, Good Spirit Lake has shallow shores which are great for kids, and the ecologically fragile sand dunes surrounding the lake provide great exploration adventures -- just stick to the paths! The park is a 1,950 hectare recreation park open year-round and has full services for campers. Many cabins surround the lake; many are lived in all year. In the winter, visitors can ice fish, snowmobile, and ski on 18 kilometers of cross-country trails.
