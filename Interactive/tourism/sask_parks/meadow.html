Meadow Lake Provincial Park

GPS: 54d 25.33m N 108d 47.14m W 1558 feet
Location: From North Battleford, Meadow Lake is 195 kms north.


Meadow Lake Provincial Park covers 1,600 square kilometers of lush evergreen forest in the northwest (Northwest Saskatchewan Calling tourism region) and is laced with more than 25 crystal clear lakes. Meadow Lake has some of Saskatchewan's clearest waters and sandiest beaches, and is one of Saskatchewan's most popular vacation spots. There are over 900 campsites to entice the visitor to stay, along with nature and hiking trails, tennis courts, and other activities and services. Being one of Saskatchewan's largest provincial parks, the area changes from parkland to northern boreal forest.
