Picture a lazy day on a crystal clear lake...the morning mist comes off the water...



an evergreen shoreline frames your horizon...



and, a bald eagle circles overhead. Then, suddenly, your line tenses, and everything changes. Your battle with a monster of the deep has begun.



Northern Saskatchewan boasts some of the best sport fishing on the continent.



Hundreds of fly-in and drive-in outfitting camps cater to anglers who swear that the fish line up to bite.



What's your pleasure? Trophy northern pike? Giant lakers? Walleye, whitefish or grayling? You'll find them, and many other feisty fighters, in our lakes and rivers.



Fishing, however, is only one part of the Saskatchewan angling experience.



Add the Precambrian wilderness, the cry of the loon, the camaraderie of shore lunch and the hospitality of our lodges and you've got the stuff of which warm memories are made.



This year cast your thoughts northward. Fish the legend...
