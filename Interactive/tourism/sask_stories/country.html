There's a special spirit to rural Saskatchewan...



A neighbourly spirit at harvest time. A welcoming spirit at country fairs, farmers' markets and fowl suppers.



A spirit that speaks family, time-honoured values and oneness with the land.



Country life, is walking through a field of hay, as dusk paints orange and purple hues on the sky above.



It's helping bring the cows home or joining in a rancher's round-up. It's saddling-up horses, riding covered wagons and journeying back through time.



Want to try country life? Why not visit one of more than 70 vacation farms in the province?



You'll experience great cooking and fresh produce. You'll swap stories and help with the chores.



And you'll sample the hospitality of people who know how to treat you royally.
