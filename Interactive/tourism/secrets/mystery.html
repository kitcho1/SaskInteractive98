Mystery Rocks

GPS: 49d 32.65m N 109d 53.15m W 4368 feet
Location: A 45 minute hike from Fort Walsh in the Cypress Hills Interprovincial Park. This is private land and permission is required from the owner (ask at Fort Walsh).


This is a strange place.



Undoubtably, people have been attracted to, and inspired by, this unusual geological formation for hundreds or thousands of years.



A few petroglyphs can be found if you look carefully.



Academics and visitors have debated the origin of these rocks without resolution.



Theories range from alien construction, lost civilizations, and geological anomalies.



Take a look for yourself and maybe you can solve the mystery.



You will need to travel to Cypress Hills Interprovincial Park to see these yourself...then hike from Fort Walsh. And, you will be on privately-owned land.
