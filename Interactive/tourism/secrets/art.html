My name is Allen Adolph, also known as Paso. I am a member of the Coyote Clan within the Stl'atl'imx Nation. The inspiration for my work comes from Mother Earth.



I began drawing later in life and I use fine felt pens in basic colors to achieve the final image -- many days and weeks go into each picture I create. Please sit back and enjoy your own private showing of my work!
