Otter Rapids is one of the best known and most easily accessed sets of rapids on the Churchill River system.



Those of you without canoes or kayaks can put on a lifejacket and swim through the rapids where even the paddlers can't go.



Some brave (foolish?) individuals even jump into the turbulent waters from the bridge.
