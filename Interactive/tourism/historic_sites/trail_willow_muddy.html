Willow Bunch - Big Muddy Trail
GPS: 49d 07.85m N 105d 08.60m W 2588 feet
Location: On #34, 31 kms south of Bengough.


This trail traversed the region between Willow Bunch and Big Muddy, providing a route for local transportation as well as a link with the Wood Mountain - Fort Qu'Appelle Trail.

By 1874, at Willow Bunch, a number of buffalo hunters and their families had settled, another settlement established itself at Big Muddy.

This trail was used by the N.W.M.P., Indians, outlaws and settlers. Sitting Bull and his followers also used the trail. This Cairn is dedicated to all those who travelled this trail.
