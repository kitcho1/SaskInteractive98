Saskatchewan Stories

The word "Saskatchewan" means "the river that flows swiftly". The province is comprised of mountains, deserts, badlands, parks, 250,000 square miles of plains, 80 million acres of forest and thousands of lakes. Check here to find out what awaits the adventure seeker.


Historic Sites

Saskatchewan's history dates back further than that of the Egyptian Empire. Take a trip back in time and follow the great buffalo herds as they migrate across the plains. Hide out with the outlaws of the old west in the Big Muddy Badlands.
